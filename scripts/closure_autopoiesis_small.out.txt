══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  • Pure closure logic (autocatalytic build + decay-DEFAULT, nothing imported)
    REPRODUCES the full phenomenology: a metastable self-sustaining closed state,
    memoryless exponential lifetimes, and τ rising steeply with the intrinsic
    control ρ=β/δ. The 'destruction' was never imposed — it is the ground state.
  • Functional form: τ ~ exp(N·g(ρ)), a large-deviation escape — the SAME class
    as the MFE saddle (metastable + memoryless + steep τ(control)).
  • HONEST LIMIT: exponential-in-size is GENERIC to metastable stochastic systems,
    not yet closure-specific. The closure-SPECIFIC test is the SCALE-UP: put this
    on the CFS K_n³ closure substrate and ask whether the STRUCTURE CFS computes
    (spectral gap / rank / F-A-S role) sets g and predicts WHICH element's opening
    triggers catastrophe (the roll/A-role analog). That is where closure would
    earn prediction, not just host the dynamics.
