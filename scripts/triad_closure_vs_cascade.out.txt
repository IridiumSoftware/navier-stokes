  WHERE THE LINE FALLS (the prediction bar, drawn)

    signed = donor) and the conservation-fixed recipient split. A closure

    return (Thread 1 → SSP rolls) are ENSEMBLE-under-forcing effects; a
    single triad merely oscillates. 'Self-sustaining until it is not' lives
    HERE — in the driven many-triad balance, not the 3-ODE algebra.
  ⇒ A closure theory earns the word 'turbulence' only if it predicts the
    DRIVEN-ENSEMBLE directionality — not if it relabels the cheap algebra.
