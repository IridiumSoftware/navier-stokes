The death-and-dying dual of closure to efficient causation

Rosen / Hofmeyr (M,R) read backwards  ·  Scope: categorical-analogy  ·  :proved=0  ·  companion: docs/ns_efficient_closure_death_dual.md

Read this first. A structural analogy, not a claim about the PDE or about biology. Distance to the Clay prize untouched. The one honest yield is the §3 / Fig. 4 diagnosis: "dying" is inexpressible in pure (metric-free) Rosen, and the layer you must add is the dissipative dynamics NS already carries. Everything else re-describes known structure (Lawvere fixed-point closure; the autocatalytic / branching λ≥1 threshold; an apoptosis/necrosis labelling). External edge-witness (Grok Φ, cold) reviewed and endorsed these as diagnostic, not generative.

Fig. 1 — Death-as-state: the entailment loop opens (Lawvere fixed point lost)

Closure is a self-referential fixed point: f entails Φ entails β entails f, guaranteed because the repair map Φ is rich enough ("point-surjective", Lawvere). Death-as-state = that richness is lost — the required f falls out of the image of Φ, the guaranteed fixed point is no longer guaranteed, the loop opens.

The genuine categorical dual of "closure to efficient causation." Mostly already in the literature (Soto-Andrade & Varela 1984; Mossio–Longo–Stewart 2009; Cárdenas et al. 2010) — recorded here as the reminder it is, not as new content.

Fig. 2 — Dying-as-process: the loop gain λ (enrich over a "strength" object)

"Metabolism and repair become weaker" is inexpressible in pure Rosen (arrows exist or don't; no metric). Enrich each hom over an efficacy object — ([0,1],×) or the cost metric ([0,∞],+) — and composition multiplies efficacies. The loop then has a gain λ (Perron–Frobenius eigenvalue of the composed repair operator).

This is the core move. It does not discover a new law: λ is a defined quantity chosen to match the alive/dying/dead trichotomy, and the threshold is the standard autocatalytic / branching one under a Rosen name. What the reframing adds is the diagnosis — dying lives in Rosen-enriched-over-rates, not in pure Rosen.

Fig. 3 — Stratified unraveling & the two death-modes

The loop depends inward (β makes Φ makes f), so it opens outer→inner. Two ways the dissipative arrow wins: necrosis (broken from outside, no telos) vs apoptosis (a self-entailed off-switch — the one slot where a genuine final cause legitimately enters).

Interpretive labelling, not a construction — there is no morphism here that "entails" apoptosis. The two final-cause slots are different kinds of explanation (NS empty because viscous dissipation has no telos; apoptosis filled because it is a programmed process), not a deep ontological asymmetry. Reading it as "biology supplies a final cause NS lacks, ontologically" is over-reach.

Fig. 4 — The meeting point: death = the dissipative arrow winning

Rosen has the closure but no metric — it cannot express decay; you must add a dissipative arrow. Unforced incompressible NS has the dissipative arrow but no sustaining metabolism — it only decays. The enrichment "dying" forces onto Rosen is the dissipative dynamics NS already carries. They reach for the same missing structure from opposite sides.

Diagnostic, not generative. This names what minimal layer each framework lacks; it makes no new prediction about either system, and in particular says nothing about whether T is total under forcing — which is the actual open NS problem. It touches the Clay prize not at all. :proved=0; no NS-ID.
