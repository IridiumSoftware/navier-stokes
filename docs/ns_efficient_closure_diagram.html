Efficient The blue arrows. The state u curries to its own generator G(u) = −P·B(u,−) − νA and evaluates it back on itself — internally entailed (closed to efficient causation), exactly Rosen's Φ(b)=f + the evaluation counit.

Scope / firewall. A structural analogy, not a claim about the PDE. :proved=0; distance to the Clay prize untouched.
